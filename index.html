Welcome to my collection of memorabilia.

There are 3 main approaches to the collection:

- List of All People

- All Texts

- Index to All Photos



How to find stuff

The collection can be viewed using a browser on a PC or Mac. You can use your browser to search within a page. On a PC press Ctrl + F or on a Mac press âŒ˜ (Command) + F. (The + means press at the same time.) This will open a 'Find' search box in the browser.

If you want to find a person, jump to the page List of All People and search (using the above technique) for the person by name.

You can search (using the above technique) the page All Texts for names, places, stories, events, or just about anything written in the collection.

If you know the number of a photo, the page Index to All Photos will help you find it.
